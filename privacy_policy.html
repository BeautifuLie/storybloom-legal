StoryBloom Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 18, 2026

Introduction

StoryBloom ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially children. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Story Preferences: Character selections, story themes, and mood preferences you choose when creating stories.
  • Purchase Information: When you subscribe to VIP or make purchases, payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play. We do not collect or store payment card details.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for app functionality and troubleshooting.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, features used, and stories created (without personal content).
  • Error Logs: Technical information about app crashes to improve stability.

How We Use Information

  • To generate personalized stories based on your preferences
  • To save your created stories locally on your device
  • To process and verify subscription purchases
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
  • To enforce usage limits (free vs. VIP tiers)

Data Storage

  • Local Storage: Your stories and preferences are stored locally on your device.
  • Server Communication: We communicate with our servers to generate story content and verify subscriptions. We do not store personal information on our servers beyond what is necessary for service operation.

Children's Privacy

StoryBloom is designed for use by children under parental supervision. We are committed to complying with applicable children's privacy laws, including COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act).

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent.
  • We do not require children to provide personal information to use the app.
  • We do not display behavioral advertising to children.
  • Story content is generated with child safety as a priority.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Billing: For processing subscriptions and purchases. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • AI Services: For generating story content. No personal information is shared with AI providers.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregated data for analytics purposes.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data stored locally on your device
  • Delete your stories and preferences from the app
  • Request deletion of any server-side data by contacting us
  • Opt out of analytics collection (contact us)

Data Retention

Local data remains on your device until you delete the app or clear app data. Server-side data is retained only as long as necessary to provide our services.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.
