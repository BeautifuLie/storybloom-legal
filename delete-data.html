StoryBloom - Data Deletion Instructions

StoryBloom allows you to request the deletion of your personal data at any time. This page explains what data we store, how to request its deletion, and what happens after your request is processed.

How to Request Data Deletion

  1. Send an email to storybloom.app@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request".
  2. In the email body, include your Device ID. You can find it in the app under Settings.
  3. Specify whether you want to delete all data or only specific items (e.g., only stories, only child profiles).
  4. We will confirm receipt of your request within 3 business days.
  5. Your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of the request.

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon a full deletion request, the following data associated with your Device ID will be permanently removed from our servers:

Data TypeDescriptionAction
Device profile Device identifier, subscription tier, usage counters Deleted
Generated stories Story text, scenes, and associated metadata Deleted
Generated images AI-generated story illustrations and cover images stored in our cloud storage Deleted
Generated audio AI-generated story narration audio files Deleted
Child profiles Child's name, age, gender, interests, and other personalization data Deleted
Story preferences Language, age group, theme, and mood selections Deleted

What Data May Be Retained

Certain data may be retained for a limited period even after a deletion request, as required by law or for legitimate business purposes:

Data TypeRetention PeriodReason
Subscription and payment records Up to 12 months after deletion Required for accounting, tax compliance, and dispute resolution. Payment details are held by Google Play, not by us.
Server logs Up to 90 days Diagnostic and security purposes. Logs are automatically purged after 90 days.

Data previously sent to third-party AI providers (OpenAI, ElevenLabs) as part of story generation is subject to their respective data retention policies. We do not have the ability to delete data from third-party systems, but these providers process data under strict data processing agreements.

After Deletion

Once your data is deleted:

  • You will no longer be able to access previously generated stories from our servers.
  • Your subscription status will be removed. If you have an active subscription, please cancel it through Google Play before requesting deletion.
  • If you use the app again after deletion, a new device profile will be created automatically.

Contact

For any questions about data deletion, contact us at storybloom.app@gmail.com.
